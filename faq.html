general questions

what is cherry?
cherry is a versatile discord bot designed to enhance your server with utility commands, moderation tools, fun features, and more. it's built with a focus on user experience and reliability.
is cherry free to use?
yes! all of cherry's features are currently free to use. while we plan to introduce premium features in the future, our core functionality will always remain free.
how do i invite cherry to my server?
you can invite cherry by clicking the "invite cherry" button on our homepage. you'll need administrator permissions in your server to add the bot.

features & commands

what commands does cherry offer?
cherry offers a wide range of commands including moderation, utility, fun, music, and more. check out our commands page for a complete list!
how do i use cherry's commands?
all of cherry's commands use slash commands (/). just type / in your discord chat and you'll see a list of available commands. you can also check our commands page for detailed usage instructions.
can i customize cherry's settings?
yes! you can customize various settings through our dashboard. this includes prefix settings, welcome messages, auto-mod configurations, and more.

support

how do i get help with cherry?
the best way to get help is to join our support server. our team and community are always ready to help with any questions or issues you might have.
i found a bug, where do i report it?
please report any bugs in our support server's #bug-reports channel. include as much detail as possible about what happened and steps to reproduce the issue.
can i suggest new features?
absolutely! we love hearing your ideas. join our support server and share your suggestions in the #suggestions channel.
