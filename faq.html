frequently asked questions

how do i add cherry to my server?
Click the invite cherry button at the top of any page, or use /invite inside Discord.
what permissions does the bot need?
It needs View Channels, Send Messages, Embed Links, and for moderation features, permissions like Ban/Kick/Manage Messages. The invite link includes the recommended permissions.
is cherry free?
Yes! All core features are free. Premium simply helps support hosting and unlocks fun cosmetic perks.
how can i report a bug or suggest a feature?
Join our community Discord (link at the top of this page) and post in #feedback.
the slash commands aren’t showing up!
Make sure the bot has the applications.commands scope checked on the invite. If you just added the bot, wait a minute or type / then search “cherry.” If issues persist, re-invite the bot or contact support.
